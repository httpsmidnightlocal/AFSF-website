Honor Guards

AFHG or also known as the Air Force Honor Guard, is one of the most elite ceremonial units. They specialize in guarding ceremonial events like Air Force inspections, awards ceremonies, and much more.

Role & Responsibilities

The Honor Guards maintain strict ceremonial standards, execute formal drills, and serve as the public-facing symbol of AFSF professionalism during official events and divisional inspections. They always turn up and perform with excellence and dedication.
