About AFSF

The Air Force Security Forces is the Law Enforcement sub-division of the Air Force. They guard the Air Force Checkpoint, and AF VIPs, and ensure provide protection is provided for Air Force events.

Our Mission

AFSF is responsible for safeguarding personnel, events, our bases, and command members across the force. We are also expected to maintain a sense of integrity and represent the branch to it's highest standards at all inspections.

Our Authority

Operating under delegated authority from Air Force High Command, AFSF enforces protocols among all Air Force personnel, with the assistance of AFLC, and ensures compliance.

Our Culture

We prioritise discipline, consistency, professionalism, and reliability above all else.

Joining Requirements

Applicants to the Air Force Security Forces must meet the following criteria:

  • Consistent activity and able to complete a simple quota
  • Committed to completing a small academy upon being accepted
  • Holds the rank of E2 (Airman) or above prior to attending a tryout
  • Must not have any prior misconduct history
