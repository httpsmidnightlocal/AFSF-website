Quotas & Ranks

AFSF personnel are required to complete either their unit quota or the main AFSF quota on a weekly basis. These quotas ensure operational efficiency and reward excellence across the division as a whole.

Quota Requirements

Enlisted Quota

Each member must guard 2 Air Force events, and attend 1 AFSF event.

Mandatory

Staff Quota

Each member must host 2 events within their unit, 1 academy event for privates, and 1 AFSF event.

Mandatory

Command Quota

Each member must contribute 3 times to their unit, host 1 important event for AFSF or their unit, and 2 AFSF tryouts (biweekly quota)

Mandatory

Directorate Quota

No set quota but are expected to actively engage with the community and/or unit command members to ensure the division is running smoothly.
